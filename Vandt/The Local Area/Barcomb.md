 Barcomb is a village to the north of Mistveil and Gallant, though it is unaligned due to its connection with the Bronzewood Company. 
 
###### History

The town is old, as most towns are, and has a strong sense of independence from the people that live there. The Bronzewood Tree that the village has within its walls is well cared for and maintained, and that has led to them receiving protection from the Company in exchange for the tree being cared for.

This relationship grew to include the Company leveraging this relationship to have the village create supplies, lodge members, and so on. It's not formally a member of the Company, but it's as close as one could be.
