#Dwarf #Dungeon #Barcombe

*This is the first dungeon the players have gone into. [[5 Room Dungeon]]* 

### Lore

This is the tomb created for *the* group of soldiers who defended the nearby Dwarven fortress. The fortress was small, so they had just one big group of soldiers who had a long history of the dangers they had bested in the region. A lot of what is written down is embellished, but has some shreds of truth, and all tactfully avoid saying whether the "win" shown actually is them smiting the enemy or just getting them to flee.

The entrance to this room has already been explored by the PCs, it has featured engravings on the wall that have been described to have large stories of them felling giant and dangerous beasts but they were unable to really reference who these dwarves were since the accolades they were claiming from the engravings were huge.

They were not the right dwarves so this didn't happen and they had to fight it.

The Spider had one 3d4 Acid vial, a Healing Potion, 20GP inside of a lockbox in its chest.

#### Room Two - Puzzle Or Roleplaying Challenge

I would really like for this to be the Monty Hall problem in terms of the solution given, since I think that that will be fun for the players and also is something that can be used to further tell the stories that these dwarves are telling with this tomb.

There will be five separate doors, each will have a gate that drop down behind it - sealing you in with one of the doors which will open. One door opens up to an actual hallway harmlessly, and all others open up to a trap themed after one of the dangers.

There will be a podium before them, with five separate buttons. When they press one, the gate lifts and will allow someone to go and stand on the pressure plate. Once they do it, the trap (or door) will trigger and then the gate will lift.

When you press a button, all except for the actual door and yours will trigger and display their danger, showing that you either have a danger or the actual door. They then have the option to switch.
