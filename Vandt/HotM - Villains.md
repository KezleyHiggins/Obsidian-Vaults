#### Chief Arkir the 'All-Eater'

Chief Arkir is a kobold that has grown to the size of an Ogre. He is not terribly smart, but is fiercely loyal and truly believes that his peoples' best chances at getting everything they want (read: all the food) is by taking this village.

He is in charge through biggest-kobold-diplomacy. He has been convinced to join the Pale Fist because he believes that once they have their piece of the pie carved out for them that they'll be able to turn their warband-tribe into a home-tribe.

##### The Deadscale Tribe

The Deadscales get their name from the chief that formed their warband, who was in service to the [[Oldtower|Necromancer]] and was resurrected from the dead. Predictably, once the influence of the Necromancer was gone however, he was swiftly challenged for leadership by the All-Eater.

They are typical kobolds, though are more want of food and drink than shiny things due to the food and drink of the surface being foreign and sweet, whereas most shiny things are shiny everywhere.

#### Thorn Quickblade

Thorn is a lowborn outlaw who has proven themselves to be capably violent to Ser Byron Blackheart. He is enraptured by Heartstealer, infected by its influence to remain deadly loyal to him.

##### Quickblade's Men

These men are members of Heartless mixed with normal low-down bandits to serve as the cover for the work that Ser Byron Blackheart is actually doing. Only those in charge have any connection to the Heartless, and the bandits here are already planned to be cut down if they won't fall in line, so they're very expendable.

#### Ser Byron 'Blackheart'

Ser Byron Blackmont, called 'Blackheart', is the third-born son of the House Blackmont. They are a minor noble house in Gallant, occupying a castle which overlooks a mountain pass to the south that pirates and ne'er-do-wells tend to use to get around discretely. 

After an instance of dueling his eldest sibling, the then heir Ser Triston Blackmont, to death he was banished from the house and stripped of his inheritance. Everyone believes (and is correct) that he intended to kill his brother in hopes of moving up in succession. His lack of remorse after the fact has lead him to having the moniker *Blackheart* attached to him, which he has gratefully accepted.

##### The Heartless

The Heartless are a band of outlaws who follows Ser Byron Blackheart. They are noticeably more keen on having uniforms and set traditions in how they operate due to them being lead by a nobleman, but otherwise are just as willing to do whatever they need to in order to get coin.
