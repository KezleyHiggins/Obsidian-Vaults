**The Marches** refers both to all land south of the [[Wastes]], and the name of the alliance of [[Mistveil]], [[Anthea]], and [[Gallant]]. 

# The Landscape

The lands in Mistveil alternate between flat fields, hills and forests - but the uniting theme of them all is the ever present mist that lingers. Anything magical generates some, and something about the landscape itself also seems to keep the mist around. 

The lands in Anthea are split between Savannah, Desert, Swamp and Mountains. The entire claimed borders of the area has some form of road, all of which lead back to the city itself.

The lands in Gallant run the gambit between Forests, Grasslands, Hills, Jungle and Mountains. Being a feudal state, the region has vast differences in how each different portion of land is run and maintained. The rulers of Gallant have always favoured assimilation over annihilation, so many new titles are often created to incorporate any newcomers if the need be.

# The Marcher Alliance

 [[Mistveil]], [[Anthea]], and [[Gallant]] are the three main states that hold dominion over the Marches. Though they are all still effectively different states, the alliance does have different parts of their policy defined by them as a group. This is why all their titles of nobility have been standardized, such as that each of the leaders of each state is a Marquess.

* [[Mistveil]] is a city-state, which claims a wide area and has a few small villages that are part of its domain but otherwise is just the city of Mistveil.
* [[Anthea]] is dominated by a city which is constantly growing outward, though it does have some farms and villages at the extremities to get resources in.
*  [[Gallant]] is a feudal state with the capital being a big castle-town, and many many surrounding villages and other nobles.

Within each of the main states are smaller factions which, while not formal members of the Alliance, are prominent enough to participate in diplomacy and have special considerations when the main three are making decisions.
