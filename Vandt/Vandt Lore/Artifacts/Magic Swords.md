
### The Saints' Swords

These are eight swords that were all created and wielded by the [[Canonical Eight]], a group of heroes (later canonized as saints) from the Old Empire.

**The swords are:** 
- [[The Heartstealer|Heartkeeper, later renamed Heartstealer]]
- [[The Moon-Glass Blade]]
- [[The Sword of Flowers]]
- [[The Bronzewood Sword]]
- [[The Maw Of The Empire]]
- [[The Charcoal Sword]]
- [[The Silvered Sword]]
- [[The Broken Blade]]
