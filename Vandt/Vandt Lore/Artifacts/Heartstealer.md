Heartstealer is a sibling sword to the [[The Moon-Glass Blade]] and [[The Sword of Flowers]]. It is a mythical weapon with its own intelligence and confers magical abilities to those who wield it, as long as they align with its general wants.  

Unlike its other sibling blades, Heartstealer is malicious and evil. It is more like [Frostmourne](https://wowpedia.fandom.com/wiki/Frostmourne) than it is Excalibur. The blade itself wants to be wielded by someone who rules like a despot through fear and power. 

###### History

Heartstealer was once called Heartkeeper before it was corrupted by a wielder during the [[Old Empire]]. The sword saw multiple wielders try and fail to hold groups of people together through moral means and all of them ended up being struck down because of their unwillingness to bend their morals.

The next wielder was one of impure heart, who managed to convince the sword to listen to them, and then over years of magical influence and evil magic the blade twisted its view to become the sword it is now.
