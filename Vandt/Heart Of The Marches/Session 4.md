

They marched on towards the hilltop and encountered a juvenile Manticore that was scared away. They also discovered treehouses setup to be manned by the bandits to give them cover from the manticore and be shot out from without being seen. 

They then discovered a troop of Kobolds who had made camp and were themselves marching towards the hilltop. Contained there was the All-Eater, who they killed in a pretty poggers fight. They were able to get him alone from luring some off and then killing them, scoring them a bunch of kobold hands.

They captured a Kobold, Arkir, who they brought back to the church. Richter, the priest, was able to tell them that the enemy almost certainly has the blade called [[The Heartstealer]] and that they should be careful in continuing onwards.

Arkir joined them. He sees Coinfang as the biggest Kobold.
