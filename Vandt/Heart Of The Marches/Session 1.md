#Barcombe #HotM

**Date played:** 2026-04-01

The party all arrived together and described their characters to each other and did some light RP. They discovered that the town has had a slow rise in attacks from the goblins that has been particularly out of character for them, as before it was like an uneasy peace.

The mayor of [[Barcombe]] was shot in the collarbone with an arrow it's currently unknown if they will survive or not. The sheriff of the town has undertaken the leadership of the town in his stead.

Matron has said that if they're helping the town they'll get free service (within reason), and Nimneri said that she'd pay them fair wage for any alchemical stuff they bring her. 

Currently there's a thing saying that they'll get 5gp per goblin ear.

The party killed them all, and then interrogated one which revealed [[HotM - Villains#The All-Eater|The All Eater]]  and also gave them an indication of their base.

The base is a ruin that was thought to be too difficult for the Goblins to figure out how to get inside, and is what must be prepped for next session. They fought the guardian, which was a big spider statue that had some loot inside of it.
