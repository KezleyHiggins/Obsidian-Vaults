
The world map is split into two halves. One is the [[Wastes]] which contains [[Glasghoul]] and the [[Oldtower]]. The other is the [[Marches|Marches]] which contains the City States of [[Mistveil]], [[Anthea]] and [[Gallant]].

The game is going to start in a village that is centered between Mistveil and Gallant. This location is intended to allow the players to get an introduction to each of the three main factions via a village that has explicitly (through the influence of the [[Bronzewood Company]]) been allowed to stay neutral.

The village's name is [[Barcomb]]. It has a [[Bronzewood|Bronzewood Tree]] and has strong ties with the [[Bronzewood Company]], or at least strong enough for the company to answer their call for aid when it is made.

### The Preamble

The party are going to come into conflict with a Warband lead by a Hobgoblin. This warband is seeking to take over this village (since it is unaligned, and does not fear the Bronzewood Company due to them being [[Dungeonfolk]]). This group is called the [[Pale Fist]]

The leader Hobgoblin, Thrak, is a canny tactician and knows that if he makes it clear that the forces slowly gathering around the town are seen as joined together then the village will realize something different is happening and will call for aid - for which he thinks one of the other states will answer in return for the town swearing allegiance.

He leads a group of Human Bandits, a Goblin Tribe and a Kobold Tribe. Each of these three groups have, over time, come to take different positions around the village in order to entrench themselves in order to be able to offer his terms for them to swear to him, or attack from three sides all at once.

### When The Party Arrives

The party arrives because the village elder sent a missive to the [[Bronzewood Company]] asking for help since they have two separate threats that they need help with, and when they arrive in town it becomes clear that a third has also shown itself.

The party is told to secure the town, make the company look good, and "try to earn some coin while you're at it." There will be a bounty board in the center of town, which will advertise a few threads that the PCs will be able to run down in order to go and contest the different threats.

### Quest Ideas

There are going to be three main quests available to the players, which they will have to figure out on their own that they are connected. These are going to be dealing with the [[Deadscale Warband]], the [[Goblin Warband]] and the [[Bandit Warband]].

As discussed above, these *are* connected but explicitly being maneuvered around in order to make it so that their connection is unclear to the villagers.
