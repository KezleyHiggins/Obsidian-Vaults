
The race options available to players can be separated into two different groups; [[Surfacefolk]] and [[Dungeonfolk]]. Broadly, Surfacefolk are from [[Vandt]] proper, whereas Dungeonfolk are relatively new to the surface from the dungeons of Oldtower. 

## Surfacefolk

Surfacefolk is the exonym given to those who lived outside the dungeons of Oldtower.
### Human (Medium)

Found in every area and state within the Marches, their curiosity and ambition drive them to explore every corner of the world, making them a ubiquitous and versatile people.

**Tenacious.** +1 to all skills and Initiative.

### Dwarf (Medium)

Dwarves are resilient, solid, stout. Even when driven to exhaustion, they do not falter. They are hardy crafters and warriors. If you are a Surfacefolk Dwarf, you likely hail from the Red Mountains around Anthea. Dungeonfolk Dwarves have equally spread across all the states.

**Stout.** +2 max Hit Dice, +1 max Wounds, –1 Speed. 
You know Dwarvish if your INT is not negative.

### High Elf (Medium)
*[[Base Ancestries#Celestial (Medium)|Based on the 'Celestial' Base Ancestry]]*.

High Elves descend from ancestors who fled Arcadia due to their people's Fey Ruler's cruelty and tyranny. Almost all High Elves live in Mistveil in big families, and were the first (and a driving force behind) to include Constructs as members of the family and fully view them as people. They also have done everything they can to include Glasskin Elves and Oozelings into their families and homes, viewing them as younger-sibling peoples to themselves. Like their Glasskin Elf cousins, High Elves rely on the Sandman to go to sleep.

**Highborn.** Your disadvantaged save is Neutral instead. 
You know Elvish if your INT isn’t negative.

### Construct (Med)
*[[Base Ancestries#Oozeling/Construct (Small or Med)|Based on the 'Oozeling/Construct' Base Ancestry]]*.

Constructs are made up of nearly anything, though it is a material hard like metal. They are inanimate creations given life, and are treated as if they were basically just another High Elf in Mistveil. While not mistreated in other states, some other cultures do struggle to understand that Constructs are just as alive as anyone else.

**Odd Constitution.** Increment your Hit Dice one step (d6 » d8 » d10 » d12 » d20); they always heal you for the maximum amount. Magical healing always heals you for the minimum amount.
You know Elvish if your INT isn't negative.

### Vampirekin (Medium)
*[[Base Ancestries#Planarbeing (Medium)|Based on the 'Planarbeing' Base Ancestry]]*.

Vampirekin are humans that have some ancestor who was (or is) a vampire. They sometimes have fangs, though not always, but always are pale skinned and black of hair. The sun is uncomfortable but not dangerous, and while they do not hunger for blood and flesh like their more senior progenitors, they can consume blood or raw flesh without any worry of getting sick.

**Bloodmist.** Whenever you Defend, you can gain 1 Wound to temporarily turn your body to mist and ignore the damage. –2 max Wounds.

### Dragonborn (Medium) 

The soul of a dragon burns within you, the scales of your body are like forged steel. You are a kiln and your heritage is the coals that stoke your flames. Call upon your wrath, to speak in the tongue of your ancestors and imbue unbridled fury into your attacks.

**Draconic Heritage.** +1 Armor. When you attack: deal an additional LVL+KEY damage (ignoring armor) divided as you choose among any of your targets; recharges whenever you Safe Rest or gain a Wound. 

You know Draconic if your INT is not negative.

## Dungeonfolk

Dungeonfolk is the exonym given to those who have lived for generations within the dungeons of Oldtower. 

**Note:** Humans and Dwarves also can be Dungeonfolk.
### Glasskin Elf (Medium)
*Based on the 'Elf' [[Base Ancestries|Base Ancestry]]*.

Glasskin Elves' ancestors were all stolen from Arcadia by the Necromancer's forces, as an act of retribution for the Fey of that area of Arcadia stealing the children of denizens of the Oldtower. These elves share the lack of ability to sleep and reliance of the Sandman with their High Elf cousins. Additionally, they have brought up with them from the Oldtower the knowledge of how to make fantastically strong glass weapons and armour that can rival even metal.

**Lithe.** Advantage on Initiative, +1 Speed. 
You know Elvish if your INT is not negative.

### Oozeling (Small or Medium)
*[[Base Ancestries#Oozeling/Construct (Small or Med)|Based on the 'Oozeling/Construct' Base Ancestry]]*.

Oozelings, like their Construct cousins, are created from inanimate collections of viscous ooze. Because of this, their size varies more than other Constructs, but the magics that have brought them to life are the same. Oozelings are all Dungeonfolk, as the types of materials to create them are far less plentiful on the surface than they are below ground.

**Odd Constitution.** Increment your Hit Dice one step (d6 » d8 » d10 » d12 » d20); they always heal you for the maximum amount. Magical healing always heals you for the minimum amount.

### Ratfolk (Small)

Ratfolk are survivors, thriving in the shadows of society where others fear to tread. Agile, resourceful, and fiercely loyal to their own, they have a knack for turning scraps into solutions.

**Scurry.** Gain +2 armor if you moved on your last turn.
