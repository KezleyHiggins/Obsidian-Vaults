The Pale Fist is a Warband made up of a bunch of different smaller groups, but the dominant from whom the group gets its name are Hobgoblins. There are also Humans, Kobolds and Goblins.

### Adventure Planning

As this group is the main antagonist of the group, and the leader Thrak is effectively the BBEG, the way in which the players come into contact with them is going to be in the following order:

Levels 1 - 2 | Kobolds
Levels 3 - 4 | Bandits
Levels 5 - 6 | Goblins
