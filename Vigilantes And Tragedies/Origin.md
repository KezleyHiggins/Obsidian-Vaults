## Aliens 

Life is out there. Of all sorts. Many have come to Earth and been welcomed by the EDA, both as agents and simply as fellow denizens of Earth. Most all of them are humanoid in shape and with some effort can appear human - or at least almost. Those born with wings or extra sets of arms require more work.

Are you an alien sent to Earth to foster relations? One who’s planet befell its own downfall and now you stay here a refugee? Are you an alien superhero, who came upon Earth as a happenstance and now have fallen in love with it?

## Bizarre Dimensions 

Fairies are real. It doesn’t seem like it, but they are. Elves, demons, angels - places that don’t follow any of the rules that our dimension does.

Are you an elf seeking to find great fortune in a new realm? An escaped demon of hell seeking absolution, or an angel cast out because you wished to help a society rather than attempt to quietly guide it, and now are left amongst the people you aren’t even an angel to as an exile?

## Cosmic Entities / Gods 

Beings are out there, with whom great power is held. To whom, you are but a spec of existence floating out in the void. But not to all of them. Some look after humanity, some use it for their greater ends and still others find kinship with them. 

Are you a servant of Ra? Empowered by a cosmic being who seeks to maintain ‘balance’? Are you, yourself, a cosmic being who’s come to Earth? 

## Lost Civilizations 

People speak of Atlantis, of the lost city of the gold and of the island of Amazonian women as if they don’t exist. But that isn’t necessarily true - at least not with all of them. Humanity was just decimated - and that includes them.

Are you a son of Atlantis? A Daughter of the Amazon, or an explorer sent out from the City of Gold to make contact with the rest of the world? What strange powers do you bear?

## Magic 

Magic is real. That much is indisputable. Before the Flash many of those who wielded arcane power of any kind were shrugged off - people assuming they were simply using it as a guise.

Are you a witch or wizard who wields the arcane to protect against magical forces that threaten Earth? A sorcerer who wields fire like they do their own hand to fight back the darkness, or a shaman or priest to a god or spirit whose power flows through your finger-tips as you serve as their avatar?

## Mutants / Breakouts

Ever since the Flash, normal humans have begun developing their powers. This new generation of heroes are all coming up at around the same time, though little is known of them. Only what causes them to awaken their powers.

How did you have your Breakout? Were you an Origin Chaser who got lucky, or someone who never even thought it might happen to you who was saved from a bad situation?

## Parallel Earths 

Alternate Earths are out there. Some where everyone has powers, others where man-eating monsters have devoured almost every soul and still others were everyone who’s evil in our world is good in theirs.

Are you a ‘regular person’ from an alternate Earth whose world is now super? A survivor of a zombie infested planet who escaped to a better life, or someone who our world disdains as evil but took a different path?

## Psychics 

Some people are born with telepathic and psychic powers. Foretelling a prophecy of the future, reliving a moment of the past, reading minds and influencing the world around them with naught but their thoughts.

Are you a mind reader? Someone who can bend a spoon with your mind, or sees potential futures? 

## Super-Technology 

Some people are super just for the sake of their abilities. Their smarts help them win fights they shouldn’t be able to, allowing them to go toe-to-toe with people they shouldn’t have any business matching fists with.

Do you have a utility belt of gadgets you use as a great detective? A powered suit or other such equipment?

## Heroes of the past 

Heroes have been around for a long time, on Earth. Many have items that they pass down to the next, imbuing or empowering them with abilities - or maybe even just a young hero they took under their wing to train as a Sidekick, now coming of age in these unsure times.

Are you the newest member in a legacy of heroes? Someone who’s taken up the mantle of your now passed ally or mentor’s cape, or a sidekick who’s now got to deal with things without a mentor to lean on?
