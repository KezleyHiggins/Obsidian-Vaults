
### Invisible Invasion

This is premised off of the idea that the PCs are members of Canada's initiative to bolster up their superpowered defenses since they have always been lacking in this even prior to the Flash. This will be based around a small town, relatively remote, that had an explosion noted some ways away from the town and has now been having supernatural stuff happen that they need help with.

The PCs would all be explicitly C-tier heroes, or not heroes at all, who have been tapped in to join the Emerging Threats Division.

**OR**

The PCs all make characters that are extremely capable operatives, so more like Sam Fisher than Hawkeye. They all pick their Descriptor and Type, but not their focus. Some sort of like, indication of what powers they might hate is given, and then during the first adventure they gain their powers in the style of the Fantastic Four.

### Robot Rampage

The PCs all create robots, who are all fully sentient but each from a different source (alien, other dimension, specific corporation, villain, etc.) who were all located on an aircraft carrier controlled by the EDA before the Flash.

They all wake up and need to find their way out of the rapidly sinking vessel, which has been scoured thing and looted, with them only not falling victim to it because they were in an impossible to reach location of the ship.
