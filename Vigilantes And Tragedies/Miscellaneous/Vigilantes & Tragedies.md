**Created: May 2022**

Vigilantes & Tragedies refers to both a setting and series of campaigns that I've run in the [[Cypher System]]. This file is here more to speak about broad creative thoughts and notes that I have than anything that would, hypothetically, help me run something.

As I've mused about in my [[Goodnight, California - Post Mortem|post mortem for Goodnight, California]] the idea for this setting came directly from having an itch to run something again, remembering fondly the campaign I ran for Joe, Slash, Nero and Toaster and thinking that I could do it even better. I remember me laying on the couch in Jasmin's apartment, watching videos about Cypher and deciding that I should just give it a try. 

When it came to inspirations, I wanted this to be a merging between Marvel and DC in terms of the tropes I'm stealing from, with the main focus being for it to have moments of realism that make the stakes all feel dangerous without wandering into something cartoonishly ghoulish like the Boys.

Here are the feelings, in no particular order, that I've come back when trying to think of new content, campaigns or the like for the series:

- Heroism should always come with a cost or risk, else it isn't heroic.
- The superheroes are Vigilantes, they don't act like cops and have different limitations.
- History is never that long ago, there should always be a feeling when a new threat emerges that if [[The Flash]] hadn't happened, we'd probably have the people we need to stop this instantly.
- When a hero not in the focus shows up, they're either focused on a different piece of heroism themselves, or they've suffered too many tragedies to continue the fight.
