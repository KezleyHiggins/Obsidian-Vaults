
This is the dimension that both [[Matt Cooper]] and [[Aura]] both are from. It has some differences with [[Earth 101]] but is close enough that both were able to join society without too many issues. The place has since been entirely overrun with the [[Zombie Virus]], as witnessed by [[Matt Cooper]] when [[Calico]] showed him this.

[[Matt Cooper]] finding his way to [[Earth 101]] was a fluke, [[Aura]] was tricked into reality hopping by an elder being that wished to save the child, only not following because they tried to save others.
