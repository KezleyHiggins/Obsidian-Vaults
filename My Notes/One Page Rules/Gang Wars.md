
**Preparation:**

Before the first mission is played, each player receives 200pts to spend on the creation of their gang. Players may save up to 40pts to be spent later, and they may only buy one unit with the Hero rule when starting their gang.

The gang must me made up of units from any available army, including the New Eden Gangs list. It can also be a combination of other armies, but this should be agreed upon by all players.

**Matches:**

When setting up the match players must agree on exactly what rules they are going to use for it, as well as what mission is going to be played, and set up the table accordingly.

There are no point limits for gangs in campaign games, however no gang may bring more than 50pts worth of units more than the gang with the lowest points value. Players do not have to bring all their units on every mission.

Gangs that have a point value lower than at least one other gets an underdog bonus. For every 10pts of difference between your gang and the next highest, you get one underdog point. These can be spent, once per yours or an opponents roll, to lower the value by +1 or -1.

In addition players will also have unique personal special objectives that they roll for.
