[[Short Story Emote Inspo|This piece of fiction is inspired by my emote of Quentyn killing Ser Bale.]]

**Theme:** Impossible


###### Idea 1

**Setting:** Chivalrous Kingdoms, Age of Fantasy
**How can I implement the theme?**

The Chivalrous Kingdoms are described as follows:
>  A collection of feudal Kingdoms governed by knights who pride themselves on their monster hunting skills, the Chivalrous Kingdoms have become blinded by their vanity and petty rivalries, and have abandoned the spirit behind their code of chivalry. Once defenders of the people, they have become little more than big game hunters and oppressors of their own people.

Considering the them is impossible and I have the piece of fiction I want to flesh out above, I think that the theme would should be integrated by having the main character feel like the act of upholding the oaths he had sworn was impossible.

This could be done through the hero of the piece riding atop a pegasus in the air, looking down at a battlefield trying to decide where he should help, stressing over the idea that he can't help everyone even though his oath is sworn to them all. Then resolving at the end as he spurs his horse onward that if all that is true, then doing the impossible is his oath as well.
