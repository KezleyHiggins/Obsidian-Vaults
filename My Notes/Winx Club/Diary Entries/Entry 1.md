First of all, I'm rooming with all my new friends, and they're literally all great! Octavia is like a cool goth badass, Mina's like somehow a jock but like the girl version of a jock? Petallia's a sweetheart, like actually maybe the nicest person here, and Iris is just as much of one! Well, I hope she does, I bet she probably was like super overwhelmed with all the new people she had to meet (so maybe that's why she wasn't able to really have a lot of long conversations?) so I can understand that. 

But, I think that's my plan!! Focus on the friends, starting classes and maybe... Seeing if something happens there? I'm going to be going to cheerleading try-outs soon so I'll definitely see her again!!!
