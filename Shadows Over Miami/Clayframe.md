The Golem — a shapeshifting construct (described as looking like a Warframe). It can copy people's forms, mannerisms and manners of speech.

Created by a powerful druid; there aren't many druids in Miami, but this one must be bigly powerful (Anouk's Session 7 investigation). It has been sighted on Normandy Island. The [[The Slayers|Slayers]]' witch who could have helped track it went missing during the First Faction Turn.

Known forms it has taken: [[Christopher Matthews]] (whom it murdered), [[Special Agent Morris]], and [[Theresa Falco]].

**Session 5:** Slipped into Christopher Matthews's apartment, fired five shots, and tried to set the building on fire while wearing Chris's identity. Morris tackled it and they both went out a window; [[Fizbal]] snatched its grenade and lifted it away to explode; it stabbed Morris in the leg and fled, briefly accosted by metal being bent around it, revealing it could turn into Theresa Falco. Anouk did 2 Harm to it.

**Session 9:** Came to the hospital to kill Gravel, impersonating Theresa Falco. It briefly fooled [[Special Agent Morris]], drew out his affectations, then fought him — Morris beat it, but it copied his mannerisms and escaped out a window. Morris is now tracking it (Hard to Shake).
