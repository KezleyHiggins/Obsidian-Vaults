# Special Agent Morris of the DOJ

Played by Darren.


He swore his oath because he believes that that’s the best way to improve society because he comes from a cop family (mundane) and FBI and stuff. He was head hunted out of the actual police for this position.

Been in the city his whole life (nearly forty)

The person who trained him in the ways of the order was a man who is now retired, who's blind and has supernatural senses, walking stick etc.

He’s different because he’s not immortal, and he was given power not born with it

He is presently investigating a missing daughter case of a very powerful rich Oracle
