The CEO of [[Open Door Associates]] and the leader of its old-money vampires — a greedy businesswoman who thinks the world belongs to them.

After losing one of her realtors, [[Harry Johnson]], she decided to push for fresh blood into the company, leveraging their Property Assets to tap likely recruits (First Faction Turn).

Her recent loss had her sharp: she saw through all of [[Holdens Inc.]]'s traps when they tried to **Goad** the Open Door vampires, which is why Holdens now sees the werewolves' rivals as worth getting rid of.

[[Toni]] cancelled a Debt he owed her as a payout from his scheme ([[Toni's Schemes]]).
