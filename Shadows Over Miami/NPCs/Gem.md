Gem (also written "Jem") — a Status-3 NPC, the leader of a new Mortalis faction called **The Purple Label** (introduced Session 7).

Sits within [[Toni]]'s circle (Mortalis) and is part of the competitive pressure around his market.

When Toni's faction move to secure a Fae Knight bodyguard failed (a Miss), Gem was the "wrath of a Status-3 NPC from another Circle" that the miss invoked. He came in and offered Toni the bodyguard [[Gloves]] instead — with wicked strings attached. It wasn't what Toni wanted; it's just what he ended up getting.
