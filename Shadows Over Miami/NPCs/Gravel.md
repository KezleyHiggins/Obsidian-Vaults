The second-in-command of [[Holdens Inc.]].

**Session 8:** The party planned to ambush him where [[Kimberly]] was hidden, using a Let It Out (Fizbal had unlocked the ability to disguise people, but failed the roll). Forewarned, Gravel dug his car's wheel in and tried to run; [[Special Agent Morris]] chased him, his shield failed, and as Gravel went to pull a gun, [[Max]] shot him in the head. A photo exists of [[Theresa Falco]] crying next to him.

**Session 9:** He survived and is hospitalized. [[Special Agent Morris]] went to look into him and arrived just as the [[Clayframe]] (the Golem) came to kill him — the Golem posing as Theresa Falco. During the fight Morris was thrown over the medical equipment; only because he let the doctors back in (to re-insert the saline, intubate him, etc.) did Gravel survive. The Golem escaped.
