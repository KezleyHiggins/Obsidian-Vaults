Dennis Johnson is [[Kimberly]]'s younger brother ("Elza's brother" — Elza is Kimberly's player). He was a young boy when she died in 1985 and is now in his late 30s/40s, one of her ghostly anchors. He doesn't know that she's a ghost.

An accountant — a nerdy one — but his customers are very suspect. He is aware of the Supernatural, and is under [[Fizbal]]'s protection (from Kimberly's story).

Was last seen with [[Theresa Falco]] before her disappearance — they had a date on the 28th of May 2018, the night she vanished.

[[Special Agent Morris]] got his name while looking into Theresa's disappearance (Session 4) and flagged him to [[James Beck]], who is also looking into him.

Has come into a suspicious new income stream. In Session 8 he was drawn into the [[Holdens Inc.]] work-scheme after [[Santiago]] called and got "the accountant" to pass on a message. In Session 9, [[Kimberly]] confronted him after seeing him living well; the conversation went poorly and left the party with heavy suspicions about where his money is coming from.
