Fuckboy magic man — a playboy wizard.

He owes [[Special Agent Morris]] for getting him out of trouble with [[The Draconic Order Of Justice]].

Is being pushed to become the next manager of the city sewers; he doesn't want the job even though the position is properly powerful.

He owns several cars; his favourite is a Ford muscle car. One of them — a convertible — is the car [[Kimberly]] received for her 16th birthday and which now passes for one of her ghostly anchors (he keeps it because "chicks dig it"). This makes him an unwitting anchor-holder of Kimberly's.

In Session 4 he ducked into Toni's pizza place (run by Kimberly) to hide from a scorned woman, chatted with Kimberly, and left $100 on the counter. [[Anouk]] was press-ganged into trying to "break his heart" but stayed uncommitted.

[[Kimberly]] ran a faction move to **Weaken Nathan Atlantic's Standing**, spreading the rumour that his playboy persona is a cover for him being a bad wizard.

The [[Winter Court]] is maneuvering to marry him to a daughter of the Countess ("Princess") to gain themselves a powerful wizard. In Session 9, Princess (the Winter heir being set to marry him) is being guarded by [[Becket]].
