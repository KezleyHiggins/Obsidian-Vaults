Bigly important Oracle, POWER. A cutthroat businesswoman and a strong oracle.

Mother of the missing [[Theresa Falco]]. Hers is the "missing daughter case of a very powerful rich Oracle" that [[Special Agent Morris]] is investigating.

After [[Joseph Savino]] fed information up the chain (Savino → her monarch → Edith), she escalated her search for her daughter and began causing trouble for Morris, upset at the lack of progress and the lack of communication (Session 5).

She has also been blowing up [[Fizbal]]'s phone over the case, which contributed to his blow-up argument with [[Christopher Matthews]] (Session 5).
