[[Toni]]'s bodyguard, known only as **Gloves**.

He is the bodyguard Toni *ended up with*, not the one he wanted. Toni ran the faction move **Put out the word** to secure a Fae Knight as his bodyguard and failed the roll (a Miss). The Fae Knight he actually wanted — the one who had shot at him — went to work for [[Max]] instead. On the miss, the Status-3 [[Gem]] stepped in and offered Gloves to Toni with wicked strings attached.
