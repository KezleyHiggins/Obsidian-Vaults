A realtor for [[Open Door Associates]]. Known as a scam artist who uses lots of names, always chasing get-rich-quick schemes.

Is owed one debt by [[Toni]] for undercutting his mark and ruining his scam.

[[Special Agent Morris]] owes him for giving information about Open Door Associates.

He died at the hands of both [[Fizbal]] and [[Anouk]] in Session 2, on the 4th of June 2018 — the pair were seen leaving the club with him. His death cost Open Door Associates a realtor, prompting [[Olivia Wilderson]] to push for recruiting fresh blood.
