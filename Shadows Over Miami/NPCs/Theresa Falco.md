Daughter of the powerful Oracle [[Edith Falco]].

Last seen alive on the 28th of May 2018 after a date with [[Dennis Johnson]] — she disappeared that night, five days before the campaign began.

Was pursuing a business interest with [[James Beck]] of [[Open Door Associates]], reportedly trying to find a place to run to.

Her disappearance is suspected to be part of a calculated effort by the [[Holdens Inc.]] people (among others) to stir up infighting between the city's powers (Session 5 theory).

[[Special Agent Morris]] is investigating her disappearance on behalf of her mother, who is piling pressure on him to find her.

The [[Clayframe]] (the Golem) can take her form, and has been seen impersonating her — most notably in Session 9, when it posed as her at the hospital to get to Gravel.

[[Max]] is reportedly dating a "Theresa" (possibly her); he holds a photo of her crying next to Gravel.
