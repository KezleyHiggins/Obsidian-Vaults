Formerly known as **Roman**, a Winter Fae exiled from his court.

He came to [[Toni]] for a new identity and the theft of the murder weapon held in the [[Winter Court]]'s Justice Vault — removing it makes connecting him to his crime much harder ([[Toni's Schemes]], "When In Rome…"). Toni delivered, and as a payout took a **Debt on Becket** (the Fae formerly known as Roman).

[[Anouk]] has been hunting/tracking him (Session 5, working with a Slayers witch). She confirmed he had left his old place and was clearly moving into the Winter Court.

By Session 9 he is established with the Winter Court: he rides in the car protecting **Princess**, the Winter heir being set up to marry [[Nathan Atlantic]].
