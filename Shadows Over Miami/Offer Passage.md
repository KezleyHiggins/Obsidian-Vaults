#FactionMove

When a faction offers passage to someone—into or out of the city—roll with their Size. On a hit, the way is made clear, no matter who opposes it; choose 1:

- The traveler exits; they are beyond reach until they choose to return
- The traveler enters; the faction gains a powerful asset

On a 7-9, the passage offends a Status-3 NPC who seeks tribute for the trespass; the faction must perform a favor—dedicating a faction move next faction turn—sacrifice an asset, or risk open war.
On a miss, the passage sparks a conflict between the faction and their own allies before it can be completed; someone comes to one of the PCs seeking help to negotiate a truce.
