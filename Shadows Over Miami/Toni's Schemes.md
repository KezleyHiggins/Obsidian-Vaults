# When In Rome…
**Client:** Roman, Winter Fae
**Circle:** Wild
**Service:** Creating forgeries, disguises and stolen identities

Complications: 
Has promised someone involved something he doesn’t have yet
Needs to steal something from a secure location

Description:
Toni has been contacted by a Winter Fae, Roman, who is currently exiled from his court in order to gain a new identity. This requires a new identity (which is trivial, relatively speaking) and the acquisition of the murder weapon that’s held in the [[Winter Court]]’s Justice Vault, which once removed will make connecting him to this crime significantly harder.

The two complications he has have combined into there being time pressure on him getting access to this murder weapon, as he’s already promised that he had it to the Winter Fae.
